About Us

BK Air Conditioning is a proudly Australian-owned business specializing in the supply, installation, repair & service of a wide range of different air conditioning systems. With over 13 years of experience in the HVAC industry,

We take pride in our reputable heating and cooling service. Our highly skilled technicians are all fully certified, fully licensed, and insured to provide the best air conditioning service.

Bk Air Conditioning is an ARC-certified and approved company. We have you covered in Adelaide, South Australia.
